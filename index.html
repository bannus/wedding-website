How do I get there?

Here are some options for getting to Saco. Note that Uber is available in the Portland area, if you want to make that part of your plan.

Portland International Jetport

Portland has a small airport with direct flights to New York, DC, Chicago, Philadelphia. If you're coming from the west coast you'll need to make a connection.

Time to venue by car: 20–25 minutes

Logan International Airport

If you fly into Boston, you can rent a car or take the train or bus up to Portland.

Time to venue by car: 1.5–2 hours

Amtrak Downeaster

The Downeaster leaves from North Station in Boston and has a stop in Saco before continuing on to the Portland Tranportation Center. The Saco train station is very close to the Holiday Inn Express and the Hampton Inn.

Time to venue by car: 10 minutes (Saco Station)

Concord Coach Lines

Buses leave nearly every hour from South Station in Boston and travels directly to the Portland Tranportation Center.

Time to venue by car: 20–25 minutes

Activities

Beaches/Coastline

The coastline around here is beautiful: check out Pine Point or Scarborough Beach, or head up to Two Lights State Park for a rocky coastline and the site of our engagement! Closer to Portland is Portland Head Light, which is situated on a scenic coastal park with nice walking paths. If you're in Portland, there's a footpath around Back Cove with views of the city. Or take the ferry out to Peaks Island to explore Casco Bay!

Portland

Portland is a very walkable city and the Old Port in particular has a great historic feel. There's so much to see, but we recommend stopping by the Portland Museum of Art, or Flea-for-All and LeRoux Kitchen if you're interested in some shopping.

Beer

The area is home to many world-class breweries. Allagash headlines the list, but don't miss Foundation, Austin Street, and Battery Steele right next door. Also check out Oxbow, Bissell Brothers, Rising Tide, and of course, Novare Res Bier Cafe.

Food

Portland has a crazy amount of good food for a city its size, so we can't even begin to cover it all here. Some of our favorite spots are The Shop and Eventide for oysters, Standard Baking Co. (get here early for a full selection and to avoid long lines!), Pai Men Miyake for ramen, and Slab for pizza.

COVID Safety Measures

We're committed to following all CDC and Maine state guidelines for weddings and large events. We'll be monitoring the recommendations and situations as it changes. Presently, we're expecting both masks and 6' social distancing to be in place. Our venue has a mix of indoor and outdoor space, and we plan to be able to accommodate guests who prefer to be outside throughout (for both the ceremony and reception).

There are currently travel restrictions for entering the state of Maine. Currently, there is a 10-day quarantine requirement, with exemptions for certain states, vaccinated individuals, those who have previously had COVID 19, or with a negative test result. Please refer to details at the Maine CDC site.
